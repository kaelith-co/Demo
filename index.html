Our Story

We believe the world
deserves to be savoured.

Wayfare was founded in 2012 by two travellers who grew tired of crowded itineraries and transactional tourism. We design journeys the way you'd design a meal — with intention, pacing, and a deep respect for place.

Every trip we craft is bespoke. No two are identical. We work with a small circle of local experts, family-run lodges, and independent guides who share our conviction that slower is almost always better.

01
Slow Travel Fewer destinations, deeper immersion. We never rush what should be lingered over.
02
Local First Every guide, lodge, and restaurant is independently run and community-rooted.
03
Radical Curation We visit every place we recommend. No exceptions, no shortcuts.
